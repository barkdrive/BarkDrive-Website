Well-being Treatments

De-shedding Mud Mask

A deep cleaning, sweet smelling mud treatment to help prevent excessive shedding. It flows with hydrating omega oils and is pumped full of B vitamins to hydrate the skin and coat whilst stimulating cell growth and tissue repair.

Paw Fizz Soak

This luxurious natural paw soak treatment deep cleans while helping to eliminate fungus, germs and bacteria. Giving your dogs paws that clean and soothing feeling

Nagayu Co2 Skin Balance

This moisturising supplement is great for treating an array of skin complaints. Adding this Nagayu tablet to your dog’s bath transforms the water into a CO2 treatment. This increases blood flow to the skin and absorption of nutrients and oxygen to improve both their coat & skin condition.

Blueberry Facial

This hydrating and soothing blueberry facial treatment has a natural and gentle exfoliating effect to deep cleanse & kill bacteria. Includes a relaxing head massage.

Dog Grooming Services

Puppy Groom

Introducing your puppy to grooming early is crucial, ideally starting as soon as they’ve completed their puppy vaccinations. Our Puppy Pamper is available for puppies up to 6 months old & offers a gentle introduction to grooming procedures and tools. We endeavour to provide a safe and nurturing environment. This service includes a consultation and health assessment, a mild wash using specialised natural puppy shampoo, a gentle blow dry and brushing, as well as cleaning of the ears and eyes, paws and pads and sanitary trim

Short Hair & De-shedding

Short haired breeds benefit greatly from our undercoat removal techniques. First, a consultation and health assessment is given. Then, brushing for removal of dead coat and bathed in a shampoo specifically for shedding coats. Next, a blast dry will help to remove any remaining loose hair. We clean and trim the paws and pads, perform a sanitary trim, and gently clean the ears and eyes. The session concludes with a nail trim and a refreshing spritz of organic cologne.

Full Groom

For dogs requiring a haircut, our process begins with a consultation and health assessment. We provide a conditioning bath using high quality natural shampoo tailored to your dog’s skin and coat. After a thorough blow dry to eliminate any loose fur, we brush any minor matts and tangles out of the coat before clipping and styling according to your requirements. Additionally, we clean and trim the paws and pads, perform a sanitary trim, and gently clean the ears and eyes. The session concludes with a nail trim and a refreshing spritz of organic cologne.

Bath & Fluff

Suitable for long haired or high maintenance coats who want a good bath and brush out in between their regular full grooms sessions. Your dog will receive a conditioning shampoo bath, followed by a brush and blow dry, nail trim and ears cleaned. No clipping or scissoring will be carried out during a bath and fluff..

Nails & Paws

Overgrown nails can make walking painful, as they put pressure on the toes and nail beds. This discomfort may lead to changes in posture or gait, which can strain your dog’s joints and muscles over time. Routine nail care keeps your dog comfortable, reduces the risk of health problems, and maintains their mobility and quality of life.

Teeth Cleaning

Your dogs dental hygiene is very important. We use an Emmi Pet ultrasonic toothbrush and a specially formulated ultrasound toothpaste to reach deep below the gum line to kill germs and bacteria whilst cleaning the teeth and gums to prevent the build up of plaque and tartar.
